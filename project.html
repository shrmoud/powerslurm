The goal of this NSF REU project is to understand and implementing power-aware scheduling as a plugin to Slurm Job Scheduler.

Major tasks include:
1. Literature Survey
2. Exploring the Slurm Cluster Scheduler
3. Running the Slurm Simulator with Workload Traces
4. Developing Power Aware Scheduling Policies into SLURM
