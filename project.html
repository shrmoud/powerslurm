The goal of this NSF REU project is to understand and implementing power-aware scheduling as a plugin to Slurm Job Scheduler. Major tasks include literature survey, exploring Slurm Cluster Scheduler, The Slurm Simulator with Workload Traces, and Developing Power Aware Scheduling Policies into SLURM.
