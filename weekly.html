Week 1 - Oct 3

In the first three weeks, I was to prepare myself for the material. In the first week, I read the following research papers: Utilization, Predictability, Workloads, and User Runtime Estimates in Scheduling the IBM SP2 with Backfilling, Job Scheduling in HPC Clusters and A Data Driven Scheduling Approach for Power Management on HPC Systems. All three of the papers pertain to methods of optimizing cluster systems between real life frameworks. I went over these papers several times, taking detailed notes to make sure I understand as much as I can from these. For now, I have no quesitons about these concepts; I am sure some will pop up with practice. However, for now I trust the authors to know what they are talking about. I also met with professor Zhiling Lan and discussed my plans and actions for the next three weeks.
